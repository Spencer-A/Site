Citizenship

How can I be a good citizen?

Citizenship: "the quality of an individual's response to membership in a community" [source]

We are all part of several communities, and it is our job to serve those communites through citizenship. However, being a good citizen-putting the community before yourself- is sometimes much easier than other times. Thus, the true test of citizenship comes when it is hard, not when it is easy. Thus, many traits are necessary to be a good ciitzen, such as:

  • Honesty
  • Compassion
  • Respect
  • Responsibility
  • Courage
  • Selflessness
